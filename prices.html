Questions & Answers

How long is a typical lesson?

It really dependends on the package you go with. Our typical lessons are one hour in duration, but if you purchase the value pack then the lessons are 1.5 hours long.

What forms of payment do you accept?

The pay-as-you-go option can be paid for with cash on the day. All other packages need to be paid in advance via credit card or bank transfer.

I want to meet my instructor before I buy a package. What are my options?

We have the pay-as-you-go option. We have every confidence you will want to continue with our carefully selected instructor, but to give you peace of mind, we provide this option for people who want to spend some time with their instructor before purchasing a larger package.

At what times do you offer lessons?

The exact times are specific to your instructor and their availability. That being said, our instructors are generally teaching lessons from 6am to 6pm weekdays and 6am to 1pm on Saturdays
