What happens after I have applied?

  • 1
    We will review your application in the first round of screening. We do this once every 2 weeks.
  • 2
    Once we have reviewed your application we will be in touch to let you know how you have gone in the first round of screening.
  • 3
    If you are successful in the first round of screening we will invite you for a face-to-face interview at our offices, and will have a test-run of you taking a student for a lesson while one of our managers sits in on it.
