Become an instructor

Ace driving school is actively seeking new members for the team.

What we're after

Ace Driving School is currently experiencing significant growth in bookings and, as a result, are actively seeking new members to join our exceptional team of instructors. Whether you are after full time hours of 30+ hours a week, or simply want to take a few sessions a week to see the joys of working for Ace compared to the competition, we want to talk to you.

How it all works

One of our guiding principles is instructor satisfaction. We keep things simple and straighforward. When working with Ace here's what you get.

  1. Only work when and where suits you.
  2. We won't force you to instruct exclusively for Ace.
  3. Have complete control of your calendar and client schedule (no interfering from us)
  4. Wear Ace magnets while teaching for Ace Driving School. Easily take them off when not teaching.

What we're looking for in new instructors

  • Patient and calm personalities
  • Friendly, approachable, and well-groomed
  • Reliable and self-sufficient
  • Tech savvy

What you'll need to become an instructor

We have the standard industry requirements for our instructors.

  • Cert 4 in driver training
  • Modern dual-control vehicle
  • Current Blue Card (for working with minors)
