What Happens After I Make A Booking?

  • 1
    One of our instructors will give you a call within 1 business day to talk about starting lessons. If you have selected a particular instructor, this is the instructor that will contact you.
  • 2
    After chatting with your instructor, you are free to choose whether or not you wish to go ahead with lessons.
  • 3
    If you wish to begin lessons, then you will be able to arrange the exact time and place for the first lesson.
